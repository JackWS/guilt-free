Support Local Businesses Who Support
Where You Live, Work, and Play.

All you gotta do:

  • Visit a participating store.
  • Open up the Blisd App.
  • Scan their QR Code/Blisd Card.
  • Collect Bliss and get free stuff.

    • After scanning, you'll see:

  • You Got Blisd!
  • Your reward progress
  • Ways to share with your friends and earn extra Bliss

    • Once you've earned something:
  • Tap "Bliss"
  • Select the business
  • Show your phone to the cashier
  • Press "Redeem"

    • Troubleshooting

    Your credit is stored in the Blisd cloud. The worst that might happen while we're working the kinks out is that your application might crash.

    If you feel like there might be another problem, the Blisd business is here to help and of course, so are we.

    Email TheTeam@Blisd.com
